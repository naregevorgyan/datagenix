Frequently Asked Questions

What are the main differences between the Standard and Premium plans?

Answer: The Standard Plan offers essential analytics and support, perfect for smaller business or those new to data analytics. The Premium Plan includes all features of the Standard Plan plus advanced tools like deeper data segmentation, integration capabilities, priority support, and strategic reviews, making it ideal for larger businesses or those needing more details insights.

Can I switch between plans after signing up?

Answer: Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. The change will take effect from the next billing cycle, and any adjustments in the payment will be handled accordingly.

Is there a trial period for either of the plans?

Answer: Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for the Standard Plan, ginving you the opportunity to explore our features and see how our platform can meet your analytics needs before making a commitment.
